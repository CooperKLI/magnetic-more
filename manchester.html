200 Years - UoM Magnet 2024

Commemorate the 200th anniversary of the University of Manchester 💜 with this exclusive magnet. This Gothic arch is one of the most distinctive buildings of the University of Manchester, designed by Alfred Waterhouse, who also designed the Natural History Museum in London. Made from warm-colored sandstone, this magnet features the Queen's Arch, the John Owen's Building tower, and Whitworth Hall, showcasing these iconic university landmarks.

✅Material: Metallic finish

✅Dimensions: W60mm * H55mm
